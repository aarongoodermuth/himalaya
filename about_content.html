What is Himalaya.biz?

Himalaya.biz is your one stop for E-commerce! Here we combine the straightdorwardness of online shopping with the excitement of online auctions. Create an account as a user to buy or sell, or join as a supplier to begin marketing your business's inventory online to tens of users!

Why did you create this site?

This site was a final project for a database class (CMPSC 431W) at Penn State University. This project was designed to teach us about database design, web programming, and database-website interfaces.

How did you choose the name?

In the project pitch, the suggested name was Himalaya.com. As a joke, we talked about designing our own project and calling it Himalaya.biz. It was supposed to be the 'more legit' version, as was obviously indicated by the name. The name stuck, and we have been running with it ever since.
